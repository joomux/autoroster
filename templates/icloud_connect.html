Connect iCloud Calendar

Apple Sign In doesn't share your iCloud calendar access directly. You need to provide an app-specific password so autoroster can write to iCloud Calendar.

  1. Go to appleid.apple.com and sign in.
  2. Under Sign-In and Security, select App-Specific Passwords.
  3. Click +, name the password (e.g. "autoroster"), and copy it.

Your app-specific password is sent directly to iCloud and is never stored on our server.
